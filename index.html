Privacy Policy for "Anki FlashCard Generation"

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our extension, "Anki FlashCard Generation", collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Data Collected and Purpose of Use

This extension is designed to be an effective tool for creating flashcards from text you select. To perform this function, we collect the following types of data:

  • Highlighted Text: Our extension collects the text you highlight on a webpage. This data is only collected when you actively click the extension's icon.
  • Purpose: This data is sent to our server to be processed by a large language model (LLM), which in turn generates a corresponding flashcard for your selected content.

2. Data Sharing and Storage

We take your privacy and data security seriously. The data collected is handled as follows:

  • Data Sharing:
    • The highlighted text is sent to our backend server (`http://103.133.224.14:8000`) for processing. This data is processed anonymously and is not linked to any of your personal identifying information.
    • The extension also communicates with AnkiConnect, a local API running on your computer (`http://localhost:8765`), to add flashcards to your personal Anki collection. This communication is entirely local and does not involve our server.
  • Data Storage:
    • On Your Browser: The extension uses Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) and sync storage (`chrome.storage.sync`) to store user settings (e.g., AnkiConnect URL, LLM preferences) and temporary data (e.g., flashcard content). This data is for your use only and is not shared with us.
    • On Our Server: Our server employs a caching mechanism to store generated flashcards. The sole purpose is to improve response times for similar queries in the future. The cached data does not contain any user-identifying information.

3. Commitment to Personal Data

We strictly adhere to the following principles:

  • We **do not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, Browse history, or any other sensitive data.
  • The data collected is used for a single purpose: to provide the core functionality of the extension.
  • We do not sell or transfer user data to any third parties.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes through the extension's listing page on the Chrome Web Store.
